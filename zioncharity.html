Solar Water Project

What is the Solar Water Project?

The OASIS BoxTM brings a submersible water pump, life-saving bacteria filters, and unique data tracking system into a single, transportable unit. Each Box can be deployed in 7 minutes (on a water source) and provides up to 10,000 liters of 99.9% bacterial free clean water daily for years. This is enough drinking water for 5,000 people every single day.

By donating to this charity you can contribute to the Solar Water Project's goal which is to provide clean drinkable water to people who desperately need it. Since 2014 they have served more then 466 million cups of clean water, have 183 community water projects, and are active in 5 countries at the moment.
    The 5 countries they are located in:
  • South Sudan
  • Bangladesh
  • Pakistan
  • Afghanistan
  • Yemen

How is a complete water project implemented?

3 Steps to Clean Water
  1. Community Survey & Water Analysis
    • We undertake a full assessment for all new OASIS Box projects including community selection, identifying a water source and WHO-compliant water testing.
  2. Project Implementation & Community Training
    • Each OASIS Box is personally delievered and deployed by our on-ground teams, and can be deployed within minutes, providing safe access to clean water immediately.
  3. Donor Reporting & Guaranteed Impact
    • Each OASIS Box is fitted with our data analytics technology to allow donors to track the location of their Box and their personal impact (cups of clean water provided).
